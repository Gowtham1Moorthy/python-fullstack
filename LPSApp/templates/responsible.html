Getting Help

At LPS, we are committed to fostering a responsible and enjoyable gambling experience. We recognize that gambling should be a form of entertainment and recreation, and we encourage our customers to engage in it responsibly.

We believe in the importance of maintaining control over one's gambling habits and urge our customers to set realistic limits on their time and expenditure. If you ever feel that your gambling activities are causing distress or negatively impacting your life, we encourage you to seek help and support.

LPS is a member of the National Council on Problem Gambling, the North American Association of State and Provincial Lotteries, and the World Lottery Association and actively supports the problem gambling initiatives of these organizations.

National Council on Problem Gambling

The National Council on Problem Gambling (NCPG) is a leading organization focused on addressing and preventing the adverse effects of problem gambling. Through awareness campaigns, advocacy, and support programs, the NCPG works to promote responsible gambling, provide resources for those affected, and contribute to a safer and healthier gaming environment.

Website: www.ncpgambling.org

24/7 Confidential Helpline (Call or text ): 800-522-4700

International Centre for Youth Gambling Problems and High-Risk Behaviors

The International Centre for Youth Gambling Problems and High-Risk Behaviors is a global organization dedicated to understanding and addressing issues related to youth gambling and other high-risk behaviors. Through research, education, and outreach, the center aims to raise awareness, prevent the development of problematic behaviors, and provide support for those impacted. With an international perspective, the center collaborates with stakeholders to develop effective strategies and interventions for promoting responsible behavior among young people.

Website: www.youthgambling.com

Kindbridge Behavioral Health

Kindbridge Behavioral Health is a dedicated organization that specializes in addressing mental health concerns related to gambling. With a focus on compassionate care and evidence-based interventions, Kindbridge works to support individuals affected by gambling-related issues. The organization provides counseling, treatment, and resources to help individuals regain control over their behaviors and promote overall mental well-being. Through a combination of therapeutic approaches, Kindbridge Behavioral Health strives to empower individuals and their families to navigate the challenges associated with gambling-related concerns and work towards sustainable recovery.

Website: www.kindbridge.com

Gamblers Anonymous

Gamblers Anonymous (GA) is a fellowship of men and women who share a common goal of overcoming their gambling addiction. Modeled after the Twelve Steps program, GA provides a supportive and confidential environment where individuals can openly discuss their struggles with gambling and work towards recovery. The organization emphasizes mutual support, shared experiences, and personal accountability. Regular meetings, both in-person and virtual, serve as a crucial platform for members to share their stories, receive encouragement, and access the tools necessary to break free from the grips of compulsive gambling. Gamblers Anonymous is a vital resource for those seeking understanding, camaraderie, and a structured path to recovery from gambling addiction.

Website: www.gamblersanonymous.org

Gam-Anon

Gam-Anon is a support organization designed to assist the family and friends of individuals struggling with a gambling addiction. As an affiliated group with Gamblers Anonymous, Gam-Anon provides a safe and understanding space for those affected by a loved one's compulsive gambling behaviors. Through group meetings, members share their experiences, challenges, and coping strategies, fostering mutual support and encouragement. Gam-Anon aims to empower individuals to take control of their lives, promote emotional well-being, and navigate the complexities of relationships strained by problem gambling. By offering understanding and resources, Gam-Anon plays a crucial role in helping families and friends cope with the impact of a loved one's gambling addiction.

Website: www.Gam-anon.org

Call: 718-352-1671

International Center for Responsible Gaming

The International Center for Responsible Gaming (ICRG) is a distinguished organization dedicated to advancing research and initiatives aimed at promoting responsible and sustainable gaming practices. Committed to understanding the complexities of gambling behaviors, ICRG collaborates with industry stakeholders, researchers, and professionals to develop evidence-based strategies for minimizing the risks associated with gambling. The center focuses on fostering a balanced approach to gaming, emphasizing player protection, public health, and responsible industry practices. Through research funding, education, and advocacy efforts, ICRG contributes to the global conversation on responsible gaming, seeking to create a safer and more accountable gaming environment for individuals and communities worldwide.

Website: www.icrg.org

Email: info@icrg.org
